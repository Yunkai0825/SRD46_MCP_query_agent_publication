Notes and context
- Aspirin often hydrolyzes to salicylic acid in aqueous solution; I therefore also checked Pu(IV) with salicylic acid (salicylate). No Pu(IV) constants were found for that system either in SRD-46.
- Pu(IV) hydrolyzes strongly; when stability constants are reported in the actinide literature they are often conditional and measured in strongly acidic media (e.g., HClO4 or HNO3). SRD-46 appears not to have curated this particular ligand pair for Pu(IV).
