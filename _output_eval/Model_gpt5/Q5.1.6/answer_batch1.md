What this shows
- The +3 state is richly documented across the lanthanides (hundreds of ligands per Ln3+), reflecting its dominance in aqueous coordination chemistry.
- Oxidation‑state “exceptions” are rare in this dataset: Ce(IV) has only a handful of ligand systems; Eu(II) and Yb(II) are scarcely represented or absent. This underscores how uncommon and condition‑sensitive these non‑+3 states are in aqueous complexation measurements.
