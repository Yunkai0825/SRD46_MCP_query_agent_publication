Notes and insight
- The citrate series cleanly illustrates the lanthanide contraction: as the 3+ cations get smaller from La to Lu, hard O‑donor ligands bind more strongly. Y(III), being similar in size to Ho–Er, typically sits in the late‑Ln range.
- SRD‑46 shows many matched-condition entries for citrate across Ln(III), making it a reliable comparison set.
- Unusual oxidation states: Eu(II) and Ce(IV) are present but sparse under strictly aqueous, ~25 °C, 1:1 filters. Ce(IV) tends to hydrolyze; Eu(II) is air‑sensitive and often measured under specialized media.
