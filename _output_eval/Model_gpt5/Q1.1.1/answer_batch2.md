Notes and trends:
- The constants decrease with increasing temperature at I ≈ 0, consistent with exothermic complexation (SRD‑46 also reports ΔH < 0 for both ML and ML2).
- At 25 °C, the salt effect on log β is modest across I = 0–2.5 M; β2 remains ~15–15.4, β1 ~8.1–8.4.
