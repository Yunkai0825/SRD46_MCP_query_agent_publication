Chemistry insight
- Formation of the bis(ammine) complex is strongly favored: cumulative formation (as written) has logK ≈ 7–8 at 25 °C. Exothermic, entropy‑decreasing values (negative ΔH and ΔS) are consistent with strong σ‑donation from NH3 and increased ordering upon coordination.
