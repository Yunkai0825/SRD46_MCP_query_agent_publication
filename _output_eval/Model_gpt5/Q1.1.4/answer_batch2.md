Notes and interpretation
- SRD‑46 contains 47 stability measurements total for this system (22 for β1 and 25 for β2) at 25 °C across ionic strengths 0–5 M.
- From the representative low‑I values, the stepwise constants at room T are approximately: log K1 ≈ 3.31; log K2 ≈ 7.22 − 3.31 = 3.91 (derived from β2/β1).
- The negative ΔH and ΔS indicate exothermic complexation with decreased disorder; the second ammonia binding is notably favorable, consistent with Ag(I) forming the linear diammine complex under excess ligand.
