- Hg(II) shows a clear halide affinity trend: iodide complexes are significantly stronger than chloride at 25 ¬įC (e.g., ML logK 12.87 for I‚ąí vs ~6.7‚Äď7.3 for Cl‚ąí), in line with Hg(II) behaving as a soft Lewis acid.
