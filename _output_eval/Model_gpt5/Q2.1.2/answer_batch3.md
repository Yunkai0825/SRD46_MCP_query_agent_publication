Notes and context
- This ordering is the classic Irving–Williams pattern: stability increases across the series due to decreasing ionic radius and increasing ligand–field stabilization, peaks at Cu2+, then drops slightly for d10 Zn2+ (no CFSE). Ammonia (a borderline donor) exhibits this trend clearly in water.
