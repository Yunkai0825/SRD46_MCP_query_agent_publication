Notes
- These are 1:1 formation constants (log K1 = log[ML]/([M][L])) for the aqueous ML species, using thiocyanate (L = SCN−), at 25 °C and I ≈ 1 M.  
- Where low‑ionic‑strength values are available, the same trend holds (e.g., Hg2+: 9.64; Cd2+: 1.93 at I ≈ 0; Pb2+ log K1 at I ≈ 0 not present in this set).  
- The very strong Hg2+–SCN− binding aligns with HSAB: SCN− is a soft base and Hg2+ is the softest of the three metals.
