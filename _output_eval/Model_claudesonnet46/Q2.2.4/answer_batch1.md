### Chemistry Insights

- **Amino acids** dominate because they are biologically ubiquitous multidentate ligands (via –NH₂ and –COO⁻ groups), making them a natural focus for metal-binding studies relevant to biochemistry and medicine.
- **Aliphatic amines** and **pyridines** rank highly due to their role as classic N-donor ligands — workhorses of coordination chemistry spanning from simple complexes to sophisticated catalysts.
- **Aza-macrocycles** (e.g., cyclam, cyclen derivatives) are notable for their exceptionally high thermodynamic and kinetic stability with transition metals, making them important in medical imaging (MRI contrast agents) and radiopharmaceuticals.
