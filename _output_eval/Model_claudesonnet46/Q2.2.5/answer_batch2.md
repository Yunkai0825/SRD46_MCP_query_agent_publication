### Chemistry Insights

1. **K dominates (76.6%)** — This reflects the core mission of SRD-46: cataloguing stepwise and cumulative (β) metal–ligand stability constants in solution. The sheer volume underscores how extensively coordination chemistry has been studied across thousands of metal–ligand pairs.

2. **H constants (12.3%)** — Protonation equilibria are essential context for metal binding, since ligand speciation (protonated vs. deprotonated forms) directly controls complex formation. Their inclusion in the same table allows thermodynamic modelling of speciation diagrams.

3. **S constants (11.2%)** — Solubility constants (K_sp and related) round out the picture, particularly relevant for sparingly soluble metal salts and mineral dissolution — critical in geochemistry and environmental modelling.

4. **Nearly all data (98.6%) is from homogeneous aqueous systems**, confirming SRD-46's focus on aqueous solution chemistry at defined ionic strengths and temperatures.
