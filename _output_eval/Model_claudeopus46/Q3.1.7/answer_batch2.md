### Chemistry insight

The stepwise log K values (K₁ ≈ 5.8, K₂ ≈ 5.5, K₃ ≈ 4.7–4.9) show a modest but steady decrease with each successive bipyridine addition, consistent with increasing steric crowding around the octahedral Co(II) centre. The near-identical values at I = 0.1 M and I = 1.0 M indicate that ionic-strength effects are small for this neutral-ligand system. The large negative ΔH values (−30 to −89 kJ mol⁻¹ cumulative) confirm that complex formation is strongly enthalpy-driven, as expected for the chelate effect of the bidentate bpy ligand.
