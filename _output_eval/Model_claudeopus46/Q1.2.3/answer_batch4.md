### Chemistry Insights

This network beautifully illustrates zinc's **amphoteric** behavior: at low pH, free ZnВІвҒә dominates; at moderate pH, insoluble Zn(OH)вӮӮ precipitates; and at high pH, soluble zincate species like Zn(OH)вӮғвҒ» and Zn(OH)вӮ„ВІвҒ» form, re-dissolving the solid. The presence of **six distinct Zn(OH)вӮӮ polymorphs** plus ZnO highlights how crystallographic form affects solubility вҖ” the ОІвӮӮ form is notably more soluble (log Ksp = вҲ’14.65) than the thermodynamically most stable ZnO (log Ksp = вҲ’15.96), a difference of over one order of magnitude in dissolved zinc concentration. This speciation is critical in environmental chemistry (zinc mobility in soils), corrosion science, and electrochemistry (alkaline batteries rely on ZnO/Zn(OH)вӮӮ equilibria).
