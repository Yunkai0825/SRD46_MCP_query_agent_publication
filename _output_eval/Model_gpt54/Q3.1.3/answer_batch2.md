A quick chemistry read: the **bis-histidine complex ZnL₂ is consistently much more stable than ZnL**, and protonated species such as **ZnHL** and **ZnHL₂** remain relevant near physiological conditions. That fits zinc’s strong affinity for mixed amino/N-donor ligation from histidine.
