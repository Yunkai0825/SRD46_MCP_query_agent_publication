A chemistry caveat: **intrinsic stability constants are not the same as in vivo effectiveness**. For lead chelation, protonation, calcium competition, route of administration, toxicity, and redistribution risk matter a lot—this is why **CaNa₂EDTA** remains clinically important even though other ligands may also show very high Pb²⁺ affinity in equilibrium datasets.
