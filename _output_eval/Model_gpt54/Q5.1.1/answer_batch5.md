## Chemistry insight

Mercury’s SRD‑46 profile is exactly what HSAB theory predicts: **Hg(II) behaves as a soft Lewis acid**, so it binds especially strongly to **soft bases** like sulfur, selenium, iodide, and cyanide. That is why biological thiols, sulfur chelators, and halide-rich media can dramatically change mercury mobility and speciation.
