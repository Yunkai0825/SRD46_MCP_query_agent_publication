| Species | Change from low I to high I | Trend clarity |

So yes: **there is a clear positive trend with ionic strength**. The effect is modest for **ML**, larger for **ML₂**, and strongest for **ML₃**.

| Observation | Likely implication |

To be rigorous, the cleanest comparison would still be **within the same supporting electrolyte/media**, since SRD-46’s network summary aggregates nearby conditions. But from the available SRD-46 data, the qualitative answer is: **stability increases with ionic strength for Ni²⁺–ethylenediamine, and the trend is fairly clear**.
