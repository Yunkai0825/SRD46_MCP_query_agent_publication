What’s interesting is the **trend itself**: SRD-46 has enough matched EDTA measurements across the series to show the classic **lanthanide contraction** effect. As ionic radius shrinks from **La³⁺ to Lu³⁺**, the metal ion generally fits the chelator better and forms a stronger complex.
