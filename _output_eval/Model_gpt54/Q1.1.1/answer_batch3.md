### Quick interpretation

| Observation | Comment |
|---|---|
| Dominant reported stoichiometries | Only **1:1** and **1:2** Cu(II)–glycine complexes were found in this SRD-46 system catalog. |
| Strength of binding | The **CuL₂** complex is much more stable overall than **CuL**, with cumulative log β₂ values around **15–16**. |
| Temperature effect | The constants are somewhat lower at **37 °C** than at **25 °C**, consistent with weaker net binding at higher temperature in these reported datasets. |
