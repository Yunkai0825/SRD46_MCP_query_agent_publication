A small chemistry note: this absence is plausible because aspirin often hydrolyzes to salicylate in aqueous systems, and actinide(IV) complexation data in older compilations are much more commonly reported for ligands like nitrate, fluoride, oxalate, and DTPA than for pharmaceutical carboxylates.
