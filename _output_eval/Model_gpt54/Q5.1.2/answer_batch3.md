Chemistry note: DTPA is a classic **aminopolycarboxylate chelator**, so its apparent strength rises strongly for **higher-charge metal ions** such as **Fe(III)** and **Gd(III)** compared with alkaline-earth ions like **Ca(II)** and **Mg(II)**. Also, because DTPA is polyprotic, **conditional binding at a given pH** can differ substantially from the reported overall thermodynamic logβ values.
