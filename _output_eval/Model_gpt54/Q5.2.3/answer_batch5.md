SRD-46 can’t answer that as stated because a stability constant is always for a specific metal–ligand system and reaction.

| Missing information | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Metal ion | Different metals have very different β values |
| Ligand | Stability constants are ligand-specific |
| Complex/reaction | e.g. ML, ML2, MHL, hydroxo complex, etc. |
| Medium if relevant | Conditions like ionic strength/solvent affect reported values |

Also, SRD-46 mainly indexes stability data by temperature and ionic strength; pressure-specific data such as exactly 50 bar may be sparse.
