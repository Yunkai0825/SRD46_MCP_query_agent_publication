### Chemistry Insights

1. **Exceptional strength across oxidation states**: DTPA's five carboxylate arms and three amine nitrogens give it up to 8 donor atoms, making it far stronger than EDTA (6-donor) — especially for trivalent and tetravalent metals.

2. **Medical relevance**: DTPA is FDA-approved for chelation therapy (Pb, Pu, Am, Cm poisoning) and as a Gd³⁺ carrier in MRI contrast agents (Magnevist®). The Gd-DTPA log β ≈ 22 ensures the complex is stable in vivo.

3. **Lanthanide trend**: Log β increases steadily from La³⁺ to Lu³⁺ due to the lanthanide contraction — smaller ions form tighter complexes with the rigid DTPA cavity.
