### Chemistry Insights

- **Amino acids** dominating the top spot reflects their immense biological relevance — they are natural chelators with both amine and carboxylate donor groups, making them ideal ligands for metal ions in biological and pharmaceutical contexts.
- **Pyridines (azines)** and **aliphatic amines** are workhorses of coordination chemistry, offering nitrogen donors with tunable basicity across a huge structural diversity.
- **Aza-macrocycles** (e.g., cyclam, cyclen derivatives) are notable for forming exceptionally stable complexes due to the macrocyclic effect, which is why they appear prominently despite being a more specialized class.
