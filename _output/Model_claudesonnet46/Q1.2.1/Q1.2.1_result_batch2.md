### Chemistry Context

This 1949 paper by Schwarzenbach's group at ETH Zürich was part of the foundational work establishing EDTA as a powerful chelating agent. This work laid the groundwork for EDTA's widespread use in analytical chemistry (complexometric titrations) and industrial applications.
