**Explanation**  
The standard reduction potential for **Cu²⁺ + 2e⁻ → Cu(s)** is **E° = +0.34 V vs SHE** under standard conditions. The positive value means Cu²⁺ is thermodynamically favored to be reduced to metallic copper relative to hydrogen, so copper is a relatively noble metal.
