Scientifically, Pb²⁺ often binds very strongly to both multidentate nitrogen/oxygen ligands and sulfur donors because it is soft/thiophilic, but stability constants are highly condition-dependent, so cross-study ranking needs matched pH, ionic strength, and stoichiometry.
