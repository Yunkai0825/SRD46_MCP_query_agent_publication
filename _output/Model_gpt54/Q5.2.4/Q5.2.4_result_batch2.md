### Verdict & Explanation

**Verdict**  
No major gaps: the subagent answered the question as far as SRD-46 allows, namely that no Pu(IV)–aspirin stability constant is present in the database.

**Explanation**  
Scientifically, the retrieved data indicate there is no curated NIST SRD-46 equilibrium constant for a defined plutonium(IV)–aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid/acetylsalicylate) complex. That absence is chemically plausible because Pu(IV) strongly hydrolyzes in water, and aspirin can hydrolyze to salicylate plus acetate, so a clean, well-defined Pu(IV)–aspirin stability constant may be difficult to measure and compile.
