### Verdict & Explanation

**Verdict**  
The subagent fully answered the question: SRD-46 contains no reported stability constant for Pu(IV) with aspirin, and none for salicylate either.

**Explanation**  
Scientifically, this means there is no vetted NIST SRD-46 equilibrium constant available for the Pu(IV)–aspirin system, so no log K can be cited from that database. The database does contain many Pu(IV) constants with other ligands, showing Pu(IV) is well represented overall; the absence here is ligand-specific, not a general lack of Pu(IV) data. A key caveat is that aspirin hydrolyzes to salicylate in water, yet even that related Pu(IV) complex was not found.
