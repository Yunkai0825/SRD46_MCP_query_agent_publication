### Verdict & Explanation

**Verdict**  
There are gaps: the subagent did not actually identify the colleague’s ~2005 citation, only a 1999 Zn–histidine paper and associated SRD-46 constants. It partially answered the constants part for the Zn(II)–histidine system.

**Explanation**  
The SRD-46 data show Zn(II) forms moderately strong 1:1 and stronger 1:2 histidine complexes at 25 °C, with logβ about 6.5–7.1 for ZnHis and 11.9–12.7 for ZnHis₂, plus protonated and hydrolyzed species. The constants vary with ionic strength, and the thermodynamic entries suggest generally exothermic complexation for several species. Scientifically, these are system constants from the database, not securely assigned to a 2005 publication.
