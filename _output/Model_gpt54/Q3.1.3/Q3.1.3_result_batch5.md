**Explanation**  
The reported data show Zn(II) binds histidine strongly, with stepwise/overall formation constants for ZnL and ZnL₂ around log K ≈ 6.3–7.1 and log β₂ ≈ 11.7–12.7, plus protonated complexes ZnHL and ZnHL₂. These values indicate substantial pH-dependent speciation, since protonation competes with metal binding, and the constants vary with ionic strength and temperature.
