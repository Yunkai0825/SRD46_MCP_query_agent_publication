So the answer is: **Cl⁻ binds Cu²⁺ more strongly than Br⁻** for the comparable aqueous 1:1 complexes in SRD‑46.

Chemistry-wise, both are weak Cu(II) halide complexes here, but the consistently higher logK for chloride suggests Cu²⁺ is better stabilized by the harder/smaller halide **Cl⁻** than by **Br⁻**.
