### Verdict & Explanation

**Verdict**  
The subagent essentially answered the question: no SRD-46 stability constant for uranium(VI)/uranyl with crown ethers was found. The main gap is that this is a negative database finding, not a literature-wide statement that such constants do not exist.

**Explanation**  
The retrieved SRD-46 data show crown ether stability constants for ions like Ag⁺, Ba²⁺, Ca²⁺, Cs⁺, and Co²⁺, but none for uranyl/U(VI).
