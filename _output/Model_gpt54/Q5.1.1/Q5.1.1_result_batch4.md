## Explanation
The data show Hg(II) is the dominant mercury ion in measured complexation studies and forms especially strong, diverse complexes with soft ligands such as halides, cyanide, thiocyanate, iodide, and sulfur donors—consistent with mercury’s soft Lewis acid character.
