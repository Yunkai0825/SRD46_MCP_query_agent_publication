DTPA does **not have one single binding constant**. Its binding constant depends on the **metal ion**, oxidation state, temperature, ionic strength, and which equilibrium is meant.

In SRD‑46, DTPA is **ligand_6356 = diethylenetrinitrilopentaacetic acid**, and the core binding equilibrium is usually:

| Equilibrium | SRD-46 beta definition |
|---|---|
| \( \mathrm{M + L \rightleftharpoons ML} \) | **beta_def_812** |

So the quantity people usually mean is **log β(ML)** for **M + L ⇌ ML**.
