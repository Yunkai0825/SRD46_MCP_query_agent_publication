**Prompt:** Is there any data on cobalt(II) with bipyridine ligands? If so, how many independent measurements exist and what range of log K values do they span?

Yes — SRD-46 has **cobalt(II) + 2,2'-bipyridyl** data, but I found **no cobalt(II) data for 4,4'-bipyridyl** in the resolved system catalog.

For **Co²⁺ + 2,2'-bipyridyl**, the catalog shows **18 total measurements**, of which **6 are stability-constant measurements reported as logK** (the others are ΔH and ΔS entries). The reported **logK values span 5.8 to 16.2**.

| System | Independent measurements found | logK measurements | logK range |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Co(II) + 2,2'-bipyridyl | 18 total | 6 | 5.8–16.2 |
| Co(II) + 4,4'-bipyridyl | 0 | 0 | — |

The 6 logK entries correspond to three stoichiometries measured at two ionic strengths:

| Species definition | logK values |
|---|---:|
| [ML] / [M][L] | 5.80, 5.81 |
| [ML₂] / [M][L]² | 11.3, 11.3 |
| [ML₃] / [M][L]³ | 16.0, 16.2 |

A quick chemistry note: the data pattern is consistent with **strong chelation by 2,2'-bipyridyl**, which readily forms 1:1, 1:2, and 1:3 Co(II) complexes. The lack of matching Co(II) entries for **4,4'-bipyridyl** is plausible because that isomer is much less suited to classic bidentate chelation.

**Source:** NIST SRD-46.
