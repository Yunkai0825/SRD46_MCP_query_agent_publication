### Verdict & Explanation

**Verdict**
Gap: The subagent did not provide the requested numerical electrode potential; it only stated that SRD‑46 lacks such data.

**Explanation**
SRD‑46 focuses on metal–ligand stability (log β/log K) and does not include standard redox potentials, so no E° is retrievable for the Cu2+/Cu(s) couple. The commonly tabulated standard reduction potential at 25 °C is about +0.34 V vs SHE for Cu2+ + 2e− → Cu(s), though reported values can reflect chosen standard state and ionic strength. Complexation and activity effects can shift the apparent potential under nonstandard conditions.
