### Verdict & Explanation

Verdict
No gaps; the answer provides both the count (109) and the fraction (~47.4%) out of 230 metals.

Explanation
SRD‑46 contains 230 metal species (element + oxidation state). Of these, 109 have fewer than five VLM measurements (~47%), indicating sparse data for nearly half the catalog. This uneven coverage means many species have limited experimental constraints, while others are better characterized; interpretations should consider species-level counting (including oxo-cations) in the totals.
