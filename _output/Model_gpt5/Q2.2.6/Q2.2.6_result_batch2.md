### Verdict & Explanation

Verdict
No gaps—the subagent provided both the count (109) and the fraction (109/230 ≈ 47.4%) of metals with fewer than 5 VLM measurements.

Explanation
SRD‑46 lists 230 metals; 109 have <5 VLM measurements, including 74 with none, so nearly half lack robust VLM data. This indicates highly uneven coverage and a strong skew toward well‑studied metals. Because these figures reflect cataloged “metal” entries, analyses should account for sparsity and potential selection bias.
