What this means at a glance
- Copper(II) has the deepest coverage in SRD‑46 by far (∼1,950 ligands; ∼8,963 measurements), reflecting extensive study with N‑donors (amines, azoles, pyridines) and O‑donors (carboxylates, amino acids).

Explanation
SRD‑46 indicates Cu(II) has the densest data and forms numerous N/O‑donor complexes; Fe(III) shows extensive hydrolysis and polynuclear chemistry, while Fe(II) has fewer but notable N/O‑donor systems.
