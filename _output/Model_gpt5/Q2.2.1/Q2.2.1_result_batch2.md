### Verdict & Explanation

Verdict
Partially complete: it reports ~85% among records with a recorded temperature using a ±0.5 °C window, not as a percentage of all measurements in the database.

Explanation
Of 79,063 measurements with temperature reported, 67,237 fall between 24.5 and 25.5 °C (~85%), indicating most stability constants are measured near ambient 25 °C. This aligns with standard practice; the remainder reflect studies at other temperatures. Caveats: records lacking temperature were excluded, so the percentage is conditional on reported temperatures, and the result depends on the chosen ±0.5 °C bin around 25 °C.
