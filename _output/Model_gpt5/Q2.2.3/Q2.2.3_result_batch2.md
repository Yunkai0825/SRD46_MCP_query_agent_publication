### Verdict & Explanation

Verdict
Partial. The answer identifies examples with >6 reported pKa values, but it does not establish that the list is exhaustive across SRD‑46.

Explanation
Highly polyprotic ligands—polyaminocarboxylates (DTPA, TTHA), polyphosphonates (EDTMP, DTPMP), and polyoxoanions (phytate, vanadate)—have 7–8 macroscopic protonation constants reported, consistent with many protonation sites; EDTA and DOTA indeed have fewer. Reported pKa counts depend on ligand form and experimental conditions (e.g., ionic strength) and, for polyoxoanions, inclusion of polymerization-dependent equilibria, so coverage may miss additional ligands exceeding six pKa values.
