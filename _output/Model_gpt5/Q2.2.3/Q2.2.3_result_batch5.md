### Verdict & Explanation

Verdict
Partial. The subagent confirmed such ligands exist but listed only 25 of 33 ligands with >6 reported pKa values, leaving eight unnamed.

Explanation
The SRD‑46 pKa data indicate 33 ligands exceed six reported pKa values. They are mostly highly polyprotic systems—polyazamacrocycles ([24]aneN8–[36]aneN12), extended polyaminopolycarboxylates (e.g., DTPA, TTHA, PHOA/TPHA), and polyphosphonates/inositol polyphosphates (e.g., phytic acid)—whose many ionizable sites yield numerous stepwise protonation transitions. For the listed set, unique protonation steps matched total entries, implying minimal duplication; this relationship is not confirmed for the eight unlisted ligands.
