Notes and interpretation
- Available species in SRD‑46 for Ag(I)–NH3 are [AgNH3]+ and [Ag(NH3)2]+; no higher polyammine stoichiometries are included at 25 °C in this dataset.
- Values above are cumulative formation constants (β1, β2; base‑10 log). Stepwise K2 can be obtained from logK2 = logβ2 − logβ1 for matched conditions.
- Ionic strength influences the apparent logβ; near‑zero ionic strength gives ~3.31 (β1) and ~7.22 (β2), increasing modestly with I up to ~4 M before flattening.
