Verdict
The subagent fully answered the question by providing the rank (Hg²⁺ > Cd²⁺ > Pb²⁺) and identifying Hg²⁺ as the highest-affinity soft metal.

Explanation

Consistent with this, Hg²⁺ shows the largest ML (β1) stability, Cd²⁺ is intermediate, and Pb²⁺ is weakest under comparable near‑ambient aqueous conditions. Caveats: reported log β1 values can vary with ionic strength, medium (e.g., nitrate vs perchlorate), and potential competition from higher stoichiometries or N‑bound species.
