### Verdict & Explanation

Verdict
Counts by constant_type are provided, but it doesn’t clarify that β values are bundled under K rather than a separate type.

Explanation
In SRD-46, constant_type bins data as K (equilibrium/stability constants including cumulative β), H (enthalpy), and S (entropy). K dominates (~60,540 of 79,063; ~76.6%), with H and S each ~11–12%. Thus, no separate β category appears in this distribution; β-type constants are included within K.
