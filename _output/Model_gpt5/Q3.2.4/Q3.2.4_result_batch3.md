Fe(II) complexes are relatively strengthened by N- and S-donor and pi-acceptor ligands (e.g., bipyridine and phenanthroline), where low-spin d6 crystal-field stabilization contributes; Fe(III) (d5) gains little CFSE, so donor hardness dominates.
