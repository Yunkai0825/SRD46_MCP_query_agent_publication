**Explanation**
SRD‑46 entries indicate La3+ forms its strongest 1:1 complexes with multidentate aminopolycarboxylates and chelating polycarboxylates—e.g., EDTA, NTA, and dipicolinic acid—while simple dicarboxylates (malonate, tartrate, etc.) are substantially weaker.
